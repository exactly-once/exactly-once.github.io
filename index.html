Consistent messaging

Wed, Sep 4, 2019 Modern messaging infrastructures offer delivery guarantees that make it non-trivial to build distributed systems. Robust solutions require a good understanding of what can and can’t happen in a system and how that affects business level behavior. This post walks through scenarios that look at the main challenges from the system consistency perspective and sketches possible solutions.
